Markdown Syntax Guide

  • Headers: # Header 1, ## Header 2, ### Header 3
  • Bold: **bold text**
  • Italic: *italic text*
  • Blockquote: > blockquote
  • Lists:
    • Unordered: - Item
    • Ordered: 1. Item
  • Links: [title](https://example.com)
  • Images: ![alt text](image_url)
  • Code: Inline `code` or Block:
